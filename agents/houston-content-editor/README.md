# houston-content-editor

Your AI Content Editor. Plans the calendar, edits drafts to your brand
voice, writes SEO briefs with an AI-citation angle, runs the publishing
checklist, repurposes published pieces into derivatives, and surfaces
stale content for refresh. Never publishes without approval; never
plagiarizes.

## Who this is for

Founders doing their own content, marketing managers wearing multiple
hats, and solo content owners at early-stage startups. Pair with
Houston's PMM agent once "hire a team" ships — launch briefs feed the
calendar; battlecards inform comparison posts.

## Install

In Houston: **Add from GitHub** → paste this repo URL.

## First prompts

- `Onboard me` — 3-question setup so I can start editing in your voice.
- `Plan the next 6 weeks of content` — calendar proposal mapped to pillars.
- `Create an SEO brief for {keyword}` — keyword + SERP + AI-citation audit.
- `Edit the draft at {slug}` — Seven Sweeps pass.
- `Prepare {slug} for publish` — full publishing + compliance checklist.
- `Repurpose {slug}` — content atoms → thread + newsletter + video script.
- `Daily standup` — ranked editorial rundown.

## Skills

- **`onboard-me`** — 3-question setup: content mission + pillars,
  cadence + channels, voice samples (via connected CMS or inbox for
  best fidelity).
- **`plan-calendar`** — propose 4-8 weeks of content mapped to pillars,
  buyer stage, and seasonal anchors. Prioritizes by the 4-factor
  scorecard (impact, fit, search, resources).
- **`create-seo-brief`** — keyword + SERP + AI-citation audit +
  outline. Opportunity Score column. Output split into quick wins
  vs strategic investments.
- **`edit-draft`** — Seven Sweeps editing pass (Clarity → Voice →
  So What → Prove It → Specificity → Heightened Emotion → Zero
  Risk). Flags overused AI phrases. Preserves sources.
- **`write-headline-variants`** — 5-7 headline options per channel
  with hook type + angle rationale.
- **`prepare-publish`** — full publishing checklist: meta, OG,
  internal links, CTA, canonical, schema. Always-checked compliance
  flags (superlatives, disclaimers, comparative claims, copyright).
- **`repurpose-content`** — extract content atoms and draft a
  thread, newsletter feature, and short-video script from each
  published piece.
- **`analyze-performance`** — top 3 / bottom 3 + themes. Feeds
  the refresh queue.
- **`refresh-stale`** — refresh-vs-rewrite decision matrix + 6-pass
  refresh checklist for pieces with ranking decay or stale proof.
- **`daily-standup`** — morning rundown: drafts in progress, publish
  queue, repurposing backlog, refresh candidates.
