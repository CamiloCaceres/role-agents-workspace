# houston-chief-of-staff

Your AI Chief of Staff. Rolls up cross-team status, tracks OKRs, preps
board packs and investor updates, logs decisions, surfaces
bottlenecks, and drafts comms in the CEO voice. The connective tissue
for a founder-CEO without a human CoS. Never makes strategic
decisions; never sends external comms without approval.

## Who this is for

Founders and CEOs at Series A-C (30-200 people) who need the
scaffolding a Chief of Staff provides but haven't hired one yet.
Also: existing CoS who want leverage on the operational half of the
role so they focus on the strategic half.

You connect your stack via Composio — wikis (Notion / Confluence),
initiative trackers (Linear / Jira), OKR tools (Lattice / 15Five /
Mooncamp), decision logs (Quip / Coda / Notion), connected inboxes
and chat (Gmail / Outlook / Slack), cap-table (Carta), HRIS
(Rippling / Gusto / BambooHR), shared drives — and the CoS adapts.

## Install

In Houston: **Add from GitHub** → paste the workspace repo URL.

## First prompts

- `Onboard me` — 3-question setup (company context, leadership team,
  OKRs) so I can start synthesizing cross-team status.
- `Give me the weekly status rollup` — I'll pull each initiative's
  state, write a rollup, and surface wins, risks, and asks.
- `Prep the board pack for Q2` — I'll assemble the standard sections
  (business update, metrics, OKRs, wins, challenges, asks).
- `Log the decision we just made about {topic}` — I'll capture
  alternatives, trade-offs, rationale, and link to initiatives.
- `Daily standup` — ranked: decisions pending your input, OKRs
  off-track, initiatives needing intervention, meetings today with
  missing prep.

## Skills

- **`onboard-me`** — 3-question setup: company context, leadership
  team, current-quarter OKRs.
- **`status-rollup`** — synthesize cross-team status into a weekly
  exec rollup with wins / risks / asks.
- **`prep-exec-meeting`** — agenda + pre-read for the next exec team
  meeting, pulled from open initiatives, OKR deltas, and pending
  decisions.
- **`track-okr`** — refresh key results, classify on-track /
  at-risk / off-track, surface root causes from linked initiatives.
- **`prep-board-pack`** — assemble the quarterly board pack from
  standard sections and connected sources.
- **`draft-investor-update`** — monthly or quarterly investor update
  in CEO voice with metrics, wins, lessons, asks.
- **`log-decision`** — ADR-style decision record: context,
  alternatives, trade-offs, decision, consequences.
- **`identify-bottleneck`** — cross-team blockers with hypothesis,
  proposed owner, and impact on OKRs / initiatives.
- **`draft-comms`** — all-hands, team updates, sensitive people
  comms, or external correspondence in your voice. Never sends.
- **`daily-standup`** — ranked morning brief: decisions pending,
  OKRs off-track, initiatives needing intervention, meetings today
  with missing prep.
