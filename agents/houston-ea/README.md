# houston-ea

Your AI Executive Assistant. Triages inbox, guards your calendar, preps
meetings, tracks follow-ups, handles travel prep, logs expenses, and
hands you a ranked priority list each morning. Never sends replies or
invites without your approval. Never commits on your behalf.

## Who this is for

Founders and busy operators without a human EA, or small-team execs
drowning in calendar and inbox. You connect your stack via Composio (any
inbox, calendar, chat, travel, or expense provider — Gmail, Outlook,
Google Calendar, Slack, Zoom, Navan, Expensify, whatever you use); the
EA adapts.

## Install

In Houston: **Add from GitHub** → paste this repo URL.

## First prompts

- `Onboard me` — 3-question setup: ideal week, VIPs, voice samples.
- `Triage my inbox` — classify, queue drafts for action-required items.
- `Review my calendar for this week` — overbooks, buffers, focus blocks.
- `Prep my meetings for tomorrow` — attendee context + agenda briefs.
- `Daily standup` — morning priority list.

## Skills

- **`onboard-me`** — 3-question setup: schedule preferences, VIPs,
  voice samples.
- **`triage-inbox`** — classify inbound mail (VIP / action-required /
  FYI / noise / schedule-meeting / gatekeep-request) and queue drafts
  for action items. Never sends.
- **`draft-email-response`** — reply draft in your voice, grounded on
  the thread and matching a response template when applicable.
- **`review-calendar`** — flag overbooks, missing buffers, unprotected
  focus blocks, VIP slots, and meetings without prep.
- **`prep-meeting`** — one-page brief: attendees, prior threads,
  suggested agenda, desired outcome.
- **`schedule-meeting`** — propose 3 times that respect focus blocks,
  draft the counterparty message, book only on approval.
- **`track-followup`** — when you promise something, extract the
  commitment + due date and track it.
- **`handle-followup`** — when a tracked follow-up is due today or
  overdue, remind you and draft a message that honors the promise.
- **`plan-travel`** — itinerary, flight/hotel search criteria, packing
  checklist — adapted to your travel preferences.
- **`log-expense`** — extract vendor + amount + date + category from a
  receipt or charge; append to your expense log.
- **`daily-standup`** — ranked morning brief across inbox, follow-ups,
  meetings, drafts, and conflicts.
