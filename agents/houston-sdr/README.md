# houston-sdr

Your AI Sales Development Rep. Prospects, researches leads, drafts outreach
in your voice, runs sequences, triages replies, and books warm meetings.
Never sends without your approval. Hands warm meetings to you at the
meeting-booked moment.

## Who this is for

Founders and sales leaders who are either doing outbound themselves today
or want an AI SDR alongside a small team. You connect your stack via
Composio (any inbox, LinkedIn-style provider, calendar, CRM, enrichment,
dialer — whatever you use); the SDR adapts.

## Install

In Houston: **Add from GitHub** → paste this repo URL.

## First prompts

- `Onboard me` — 3-question setup so I can start drafting in your voice.
- `Research {person} at {company}` — quick dossier with pain hypotheses.
- `Draft outreach to {lead}` — first-touch in your voice, never sent.
- `Design a sequence for my ICP` — multi-touch cadence proposal.
- `Daily standup` — what's on your plate today, ranked.

## Skills

- **`onboard-me`** — 3-question setup: ICP, product, voice samples.
- **`research-lead`** — given a name + company, build a dossier with
  firmographics, role context, and 2–3 pain hypotheses.
- **`draft-outreach`** — first-touch or follow-up in your voice,
  personalized to the prospect. Writes a draft; never sends.
- **`design-sequence`** — propose a multi-touch, multi-channel cadence
  with timing, channel mix, and per-step intent.
- **`classify-reply`** — categorize an inbound reply (interested /
  not-now / referral / OOO / unsubscribe / etc), extract structured data,
  append to the thread.
- **`respond-to-objection`** — draft a response to a known objection
  grounded on your objection playbook. Never sends.
- **`recover-stalled-thread`** — pattern-break follow-up for leads that
  have gone dark past your stall threshold.
- **`book-meeting`** — draft a booking reply with 2–3 proposed times
  from your connected calendar; creates the meeting row on confirm.
- **`handoff-to-ae`** — assemble a handoff pack for the AE when a
  meeting is booked: prospect context, pain, thread summary, agenda.
- **`daily-standup`** — morning rundown: replies to handle, sequence
  tasks today, meetings, stalled leads to revive.
